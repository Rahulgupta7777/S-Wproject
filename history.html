Space exploration has been humanity's quest to understand the cosmos and its origins. From the invention of the telescope to sending rovers to Mars, every step has been a leap toward uncovering the universe's mysteries.

Milestones in Space Exploration

  • 1610: First telescopic observation of the night sky, discovery of the Galilean moons.
      By Galileo Galilei
  • 1688: First reflecting telescope by Isaac Newton.
  • 1957: Launch of the first artificial satellite, Sputnik 1.
      By Soviet Union
  • 1969: First manned moon landing during Apollo 11.
      By NASA
  • 1990: Launch of the Hubble Space Telescope, providing deep-space imagery.
      By NASA and ESA
  • 2021: Landing of Perseverance Rover on Mars to explore signs of ancient life.
      By NASA
